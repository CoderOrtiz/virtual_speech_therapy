Teletherapy

Speak Up provides teletherapy services. This means everything is conducted "virtually" and there is NO physical clinic location. Speak Up utilizes a secure (HIPAA-compliant) software program. All services are conducted through this software program in order to ensure your privacy is never compromised. After you schedule your appointment, you will receive an email with a link to your client portal where you can access the virtual clinic room, send direct messages to your therapist, fill out forms, make payments, and change your appointment day/time as needed. The virtual clinic room and client portal is accessible through desktop computers, laptops with webcams, tablets, and smart phones!

Adults & Seniors

We evaluate and treat adults with a variety of voice, speech, language, swallowing, and cognitive communication disorders that may arise from neurogenic disorders and injuries such as stroke, traumatic brain injury, seizures, Alzheimer's disease, Parkinson's disease, Multiple Sclerosis and more. Other causes may include, vocal cord paralysis, muscle tension dysphonia, vocal polyps/nodules, Head & Neck Cancers, and presbyphonia (aging voice).

Pediatrics

We also evaluate and treat pediatric clients with speech, language, and pragmatic communication disorders. We utilized standardized treatment tools designed to be administered over our virtual platform to develop individualized treatment plans for each child. We work to create a fun, yet effective care plan for each kiddo by considering their unique personality and interests in order to keep them engaged and motivated to participate in treatment. We also believe parental involvement is crucial for transparency regarding anticipated outcomes as well as ensuring therapy gains are generalized outside of individual treatment sessions.
