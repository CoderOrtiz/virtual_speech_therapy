Teletherapy

Speak Up provides teletherapy services. This means everything is conducted "virtually" and there is NO physical clinic location. Speak Up utilizes a secure (HIPAA-compliant) software program. All services are conducted through this software program in order to ensure your privacy is never compromised. After you schedule your appointment, you will receive an email with a link to your client portal where you can access the virtual clinic room, send direct messages to your therapist, fill out forms, make payments, and change your appointment day/time as needed. The virtual clinic room and client portal is accessible through desktop computers, laptops with webcams, tablets, and smart phones!
